Frequently asked questions

  • Yes, Adobe offers free products like Acrobat Reader, Fill & Sign, Photoshop Express, and Adobe Scan. You can also use Creative Cloud Express, Fresco, and Lightroom Mobile for free, with the option of making in-app purchases.

  • Adobe products are industry-standard tools used by professionals worldwide. Whether you're creating content, editing PDFs, or managing digital documents, Adobe's ecosystem offers deep capabilities, cloud storage, and cross-device access that make them a strong value for creative and business users alike.

  • Acrobat Pro is Adobe's most powerful PDF editor, with 70+ tools for editing, converting, signing, and protecting documents. For teams that also need AI-powered insights and creative tools, Acrobat Studio adds AI Assistant and Adobe Express Premium on top of the full Pro feature set.

  • Adobe offers more than 20 products across Creative Cloud, Document Cloud, and Experience Cloud — spanning photography, design, video, illustration, 3D, PDF, e-signatures, and enterprise marketing technology.

  • Pricing varies by product and plan type. Individual apps start at a low monthly rate, while all-apps bundles and team plans are priced per license. Students and teachers qualify for significant discounts. See the plans above for current pricing.

  • Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Acrobat, and Adobe Express are among Adobe's most widely used products. Acrobat Reader remains one of the most downloaded free apps globally, with hundreds of millions of users.

  • Students and teachers at accredited institutions are eligible for exclusive discounted pricing. You'll need to verify your eligibility through SheerID at checkout. The discount applies to individual plans only — not team or business plans.

  • Adobe Substance 3D is Adobe's suite for 3D design and visualization, including Substance Painter for texturing, Substance Designer for material creation, and Stager for rendering. It's used across games, film, and product design.

  • Adobe Illustrator is the industry standard for logo and vector design. Its precise drawing tools, scalable file format, and rich typography controls make it the go-to app for brand identity work at any scale.

  • Adobe Premiere Pro is the leading professional video editor, used in film, television, and online content production. For motion graphics and visual effects, After Effects pairs seamlessly with Premiere Pro as part of Creative Cloud.
