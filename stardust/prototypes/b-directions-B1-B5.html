B5 · EDITORIAL PACE

Slow + type-led.

Scroll cinema at the speed of a Sunday newspaper, not a SaaS demo. Hero held longer (400vh container). easeOut3 → easeOut5. Body line-height generous (1.7). Lead paragraphs picked up to weight 500. Reading paces with the motion.

Below the hero

A page that paces with the reader.

Stories don't pan on flinch; the mouse must dwell 100ms before the track responds. The tutorial carousel's reverse-hero entrance dilates from 1.6s to 2.4s. The garage-door reveal extends. Each motion lasts longer than the reader expects, which is the point.

Reading paces with motion. The hero holds for an extra heartbeat. The body type sits at 1.7 line-height. The brand exhales between paragraphs.
